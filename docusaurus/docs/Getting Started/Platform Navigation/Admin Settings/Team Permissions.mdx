Team permissions allows you to share resources to your teams

## Navigating to the Team Permissions
Only Admin users can modify the team settings. 

To access the Team Permissions:
1. Open up the side menu. Click on Settings.

![SettingsButton](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/Settings.png)

2. Once on the Settings page, enable the Admin mode. 

![Enable Admin Mode](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/Admin%20On.png)

3. Click on the **Team Permissions** tile.

![Enable Admin Mode](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/TeamPermissions.png)

## Adding a New Team

1.Click the "Add Team" button to continue.
![Enable Admin Mode](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/addteam.png)

2.Fill in the details that suit your preferences.
![Enable Admin Mode](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/adddetails.png)

3.Select the team type according to your requirement.
    - **Custom**: Teams are managed directly within your application. Users manually invite, add, or remove members, and all authentication is handled by your platform.
    - **Your Organization Login**: Teams are managed via an external identity provider, specifically Microsoft. Members authenticate using their Microsoft accounts.
    - **API Login**: Teams are managed and authenticated via an API integration with an external identity or authentication provider.

![Enable Admin Mode](../../../../static/autoImg/PlatformNavigation/Admin%20Query/teamtypes.png)

4.Once all details are filled in, select "Add" to create a new team.
